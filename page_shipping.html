Shipping Information

We aim to process orders within 1-3 business days.

Once shipped, delivery times typically range between 6-12 business days depending on destination, local carrier performance, and seasonal demand.

Order tracking

Once your order is on the way, you’ll receive a tracking number by email so you can follow its progress.

Please note

  • Delivery times may vary during peak periods, holidays, or due to customs delays.
  • Orders are fulfilled as quickly as possible, but shipping timelines are estimates and not guarantees.
  • Please double-check your shipping address before placing your order.

Questions?

If you have any shipping questions, contact us via the Contact page and we’ll help you out.
