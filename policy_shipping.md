# Shipping Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Processing Time

We aim to process all orders within **1–3 business days** after payment confirmation.

## Delivery Time

Once shipped, delivery typically takes **6–12 business days** depending on:
- Destination
- Local carrier performance
- Customs processing (for international orders)
- Seasonal demand

These are estimates, not guarantees.

## Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number so you can follow your shipment's progress.

If you do not receive tracking within 3 business days of placing your order, please contact us.

## Shipping Delays

Delivery times may be affected by:
- Peak holiday periods
- Weather conditions
- Customs delays
- Carrier disruptions

We fulfill orders as quickly as possible, but we cannot guarantee exact delivery dates.

## Lost or Stolen Packages

If your tracking shows the package was delivered but you did not receive it:
- Check with neighbors or building management
- Confirm the shipping address was correct
- Contact us within 7 days of the delivery date

We will work with you to resolve the issue.

## International Orders

For orders outside the United States:
- Delivery times may be longer
- You may be responsible for customs fees, duties, or import taxes
- These fees are not included in your order total

## Address Accuracy

Please double-check your shipping address before placing your order. We are not responsible for delays or non-delivery caused by incorrect or incomplete addresses.

## Questions?

For shipping questions, visit our Contact page or email us with your order number.
