## KEYWORD STRATEGY NOTES

### ✅ DO:
- Use ALL 7 keyword slots (don't waste any)
- Include your unique angle (killer clown, Florida, DNA)
- Mix broad terms (true crime) + specific (Marlene Warren)
- Use 3-4 word phrases (not single words)

### ❌ DON'T:
- Use competitor book titles or author names
- Repeat words already in your book title/subtitle
- Waste characters on single words ("crime", "murder")
- Use overly generic terms without context ("thriller")
- Ignore geographic terms (Florida = built-in audience)
