# Homepage Residual Cleanup Checklist

Use this after a homepage/theme rewrite for a single-product self-care store.

## 1) CTA Links
- Check every primary CTA (`Shop now`, `Buy now`, `Learn more`, sticky buttons, image banners, trust bar buttons).
- Confirm all CTAs land on the current product page, bundle page, or intended anchor — not old collections, draft pages, or `/products/...` handles from the previous brand.
- Test header, hero, comparison table, FAQ, footer, announcement bar, and mobile-only buttons separately.
- Make sure buttons in hidden/duplicated sections are not still live with stale links.

## 2) Product Naming & Copy Leftovers
- Search homepage sections for old product names, old bundle names, previous SKU nicknames, and retired taglines.
- Check headings, subheadings, button labels, alt text, badges, captions, popups, and metafield-driven snippets.
- Verify ingredients/material claims, usage claims, and size/pack references still match the current product.
- Look for posture-corrector or posture-device wording accidentally left from an earlier store/theme.

## 3) Testimonials & Reviews
- Confirm testimonials actually describe the current self-care product, not a different category/problem.
- Remove quotes mentioning wrong pain points, product form factor, or impossible outcomes.
- Check reviewer names, star widgets, before/after captions, and review app blocks for mismatches.
- Verify any imported review carousel is filtered to the correct product.

## 4) Images, Icons & Media
- Audit hero images, lifestyle images, comparison graphics, icon-with-text blocks, and mobile-specific images.
- Remove old product photography, packaging shots, or diagrams from the previous brand/theme version.
- Check image alt text and filenames for outdated product references.
- Confirm trust badges, guarantee graphics, and press logos are current and visually consistent.

## 5) Stray or Hidden Sections
- Review theme customizer for disabled, duplicated, or off-screen sections left behind during the rewrite.
- Check for old announcement bars, promo strips, feature rows, FAQs, blog teasers, and embedded app blocks.
- Preview desktop and mobile: sometimes an old section only appears on one breakpoint.
- Inspect footer and secondary menus for old landing pages, policies, or campaign pages.

## 6) Trust Bar / Guarantee Consistency
- Make sure shipping, guarantee, returns, secure checkout, and support messaging match current policy.
- Keep wording consistent across hero trust bar, product highlights, cart note, and footer.
- Verify timing claims like `2-5 day shipping` or `30-night guarantee` are not contradicted elsewhere.
- Check icons and badge order so trust elements feel intentional, not patched together.

## 7) Leftover Posture-Product References
- Search theme content for terms like `posture`, `corrector`, `back brace`, `alignment`, `slouch`, and old product handles.
- Check homepage JSON/templates, section settings, translations, and app embeds for hidden leftovers.
- Review comparison charts and FAQ answers for references to posture improvement if the store now sells a different self-care product.
- Confirm schema markup, SEO title/description snippets, and social share text don't reference the old posture product.

## 8) Final Pass
- Click through the homepage on desktop and mobile as a new customer.
- Run a store-wide text search for old brand/product terms before publishing.
- Have one person unfamiliar with the rewrite do a 5-minute mismatch sweep.
- Save a punch list of anything still inconsistent so fixes can be batched in one pass.
