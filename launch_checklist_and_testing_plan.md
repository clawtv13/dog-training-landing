## Soft Launch Timeline (First 24–48 Hours)

**Soft launch = unpaid traffic only, no ads, limited promotion**

### Goal
- Verify checkout works end-to-end
- Catch bugs, UX friction before spending ad budget
- Gather baseline metrics

### Activities
1. **Tell internal circle** (friends, family, close network)
   - Share store link privately
   - Ask for feedback (copy, UX, checkout)
   - Request test purchases

2. **Monitor real-time**
   - Watch Shopify admin for order notifications
   - Check Stripe for successful charges
   - Monitor email confirmations
   - Check Google Analytics for traffic, device mix, page duration

3. **Fix immediately**
   - Any checkout errors → fix before ads launch
   - Any copy typos → fix immediately
   - Any image loading issues → fix immediately

4. **Gather feedback**
   - Ask test purchasers: Was checkout easy? Anything confusing?
   - Did order confirmation email arrive?
   - How does product look in person?

### Success Criteria
- ✓ 3–5 successful test orders completed
- ✓ No checkout errors
- ✓ Order confirmation emails sent
- ✓ Stripe payments successful
- ✓ Google Analytics tracking orders
- ✓ No critical bugs found

## Post-Launch A/B Testing Plan (Weeks 3+)

### Test Schedule: Weekly iterations

### Week 3: Homepage/Landing Page Variations

**Test 1: Hero Headline**
- **Control:** "Cool Your Head. Calm Your Day."
- **Variant A:** "15-Minute Reset from Heat & Stress"
- **Variant B:** "No Cords. No Fuss. Just Instant Cooling."
- **Metric:** Homepage CTR to product page, time on page
- **Duration:** 1 week, 50/25/25 traffic split
- **Sample size:** 2000+ visitors
- **Winner:** Declare winner at 95% confidence

**Test 2: Hero Image/Video**
- **Control:** Current image/video
- **Variant A:** Product close-up + person wearing it
- **Variant B:** Lifestyle (person relaxing with cap)
- **Metric:** CTR, bounce rate, conversion rate
- **Duration:** 1 week
- **Sample size:** 1000+ visitors

### Week 4: Product Page Copy

**Test 1: Above-Fold Section**
- **Control:** Current 3-bullet benefit section
- **Variant A:** "Why It Works" section with 5 short benefits
- **Variant B:** Social proof + benefits combined
- **Metric:** Scroll depth, add-to-cart rate
- **Duration:** 1 week, 50/25/25 split
- **Sample size:** Product page visitors

**Test 2: CTA Button Text**
- **Control:** "Add to Cart"
- **Variant A:** "Get Cooling Relief"
- **Variant B:** "Buy Now — 30-Day Guarantee"
- **Metric:** Add-to-cart rate, average order value
- **Duration:** 1 week
- **Sample size:** 500+ product page visitors

### Week 5: Checkout Page

**Test 1: Trust Signals**
- **Control:** Standard trust bar above cart
- **Variant A:** Trust bar + exit popup with discount
- **Variant B:** Trust bar + live customer count
- **Metric:** Cart abandonment rate, conversion rate
- **Duration:** 1 week
- **Sample size:** 500+ add-to-cart events

**Test 2: Discount Field**
- **Control:** Optional discount code field
- **Variant A:** "Have a code? Enter below" (pre-filled option)
- **Variant B:** No discount field visible (encourage full-price purchase)
- **Metric:** Average order value, conversion rate, discount usage
- **Duration:** 1 week

### Week 6: Email Subject Lines

**Test 1: Welcome Email**
- **Control:** "Welcome to Calmora ✓"
- **Variant A:** "Your reset is on the way"
- **Variant B:** "Get the most out of your Cooling Relief Cap"
- **Metric:** Open rate, click rate
- **Duration:** Minimum 500 sends (may take 2–3 weeks)
- **Sample size:** 50/25/25 split

**Test 2: Post-Delivery Check-In**
- **Control:** "How's your reset going? ✨"
- **Variant A:** "We want to know what you think"
- **Variant B:** "[First Name], did your package arrive?"
- **Metric:** Open rate, reply rate
- **Duration:** Continuous (minimum 300 sends to declare winner)

## Scaling Checklist (After 30 Days)

**Only scale if these conditions are met:**

- [ ] ROAS is consistently 2:1 or better
- [ ] CAC is sustainable (no more than 30% of first-order value)
- [ ] Conversion rate is 1%+ (visitor → order)
- [ ] Product quality and customer satisfaction are high (minimal refunds, good reviews)
- [ ] Email sequences are generating positive ROI
- [ ] You can reinvest profits into ads (at least $1–2k budget)

**Scaling actions:**
1. Increase daily ad budget by 50% (TikTok winner + Meta winner)
2. Add new audience segment or lookalike audience
3. Launch new creative variant (test next angle)
4. Expand to third platform (Google Shopping, Pinterest, if applicable)
5. Consider influencer partnerships ($500–1000 budget)

---

## Red Flags (Pause & Investigate)

- [ ] Conversion rate drops below 0.5% suddenly
- [ ] ROAS falls below 1:1 for 3 consecutive days
- [ ] Customer complaints spike or reviews tank
- [ ] Email unsubscribe rate exceeds 1%
- [ ] Website errors or checkout failures reported
- [ ] Ad account flagged for policy violation
- [ ] High refund/return rate (>20%)

**Action:** Pause ads, audit copy/product/checkout, fix issues, then relaunch.
