### Opción B: Quiz (MEJOR)

**"What's Destroying Your Sleep? Take the Quiz"**

**Por qué funciona mejor:**
- ✅ Engagement alto (interactivo)
- ✅ Conversión 40-60% (vs 30-50% PDF)
- ✅ Segmentación automática
- ✅ Personalización del funnel

**Estructura del quiz:**

1. **"How long does it take you to fall asleep?"**
   - Under 15 min
   - 15-30 min
   - 30-60 min
   - Over 1 hour

2. **"Do you wake up during the night?"**
   - Never
   - 1-2 times
   - 3-5 times
   - Constantly

3. **"How do you feel in the morning?"**
   - Energized
   - Tired but functional
   - Exhausted
   - Like I didn't sleep

4. **"What's your biggest sleep struggle?"**
   - Can't fall asleep
   - Wake up at 3am
   - Wake up exhausted
   - Anxiety at night

5. **"Have you tried supplements?"**
   - Never
   - Tried but didn't work
   - Currently using
   - Prefer natural methods

**Resultado:** "You're a [Sleep Type]. Here's your personalized protocol."

**Email capture:** "Enter email to get your results + 7-day protocol"
