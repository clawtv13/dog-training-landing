# Return Policy - Cooling Relief Cap

Returns & Exchanges - Cooling Relief Cap

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Cooling Relief Cap. If for any reason you are not happy with your purchase, we offer returns and exchanges under the following conditions.

**Eligibility for Returns:**
*   Items must be returned within **30 days** of the original purchase date.
*   Items must be in their original, unused condition, with all original tags and packaging intact.
*   Personalized or final sale items are not eligible for return.

**How to Initiate a Return/Exchange:**
1.  Contact our customer support team at support@coolingreliefcap.com with your order number and the reason for your return/exchange.
2.  We will provide you with instructions for returning the item.
3.  Customers are responsible for return shipping costs, unless the item received was faulty or incorrect.

**Refunds:**
Once we receive your returned item and it is inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will be applied to your original method of payment within 5-10 business days.

**Exchanges:**
If you wish to exchange an item for a different color or variation, please follow the return process and place a new order for the desired item.

**Faulty or Incorrect Items:**
If you received a faulty product or an incorrect item, please contact us immediately at support@coolingreliefcap.com with photographic evidence. We will arrange for a replacement or refund, including return shipping costs.
