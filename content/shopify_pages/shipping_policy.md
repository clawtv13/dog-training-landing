# Shipping Policy - Cooling Relief Cap

Shipping Information - Cooling Relief Cap

At Cooling Relief Cap, we strive to get your order to you as quickly and efficiently as possible.

**Processing Time:**
Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding weekends and holidays) after your order is confirmed. You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been successfully placed.

**Shipping Methods & Estimated Delivery Times:**
We offer several shipping options to meet your needs. Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on your location, carrier delays, and order volume.

*   **Standard Shipping:** (3-7 business days) - Estimated flat rate or calculated at checkout.
*   **Expedited Shipping:** (2-3 business days) - Calculated at checkout.
*   **International Shipping:** (7-21 business days+) - Calculated at checkout. Please be aware that international orders may be subject to customs duties, taxes, and fees upon arrival, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

**Shipping Costs:**
Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight of your order and your destination. You will see the exact shipping costs during the checkout process before finalizing your order.

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package directly on the carrier's website.
