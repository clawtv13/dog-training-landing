### YouTube Description:

A deep psychological dive into gaslighting - the invisible form of abuse that makes you question your own sanity.

🌀 What is Gaslighting?
Gaslighting is a form of psychological manipulation where the abuser makes the victim question their own memory, perception, and sanity. It's named after the 1944 film "Gaslight" where a husband manipulates his wife into believing she's going insane.

🧠 The Three Stages:
1. **Disorientation** - Small contradictions that make you doubt yourself
2. **Defense** - You try to prove you're right but evidence seems to vanish
3. **Depression** - You stop trusting your own perception of reality

⚠️ Warning Signs of Gaslighting:
- They deny things they clearly said or did
- They tell you you're "too sensitive" or "crazy"
- Your memories are consistently "wrong" according to them
- You constantly second-guess yourself
- You apologize even when you're not sure what you did
- You make excuses for their behavior to others

🛡️ How to Protect Yourself:
- Keep a private journal of events and conversations
- Trust your gut feelings
- Maintain connections with friends/family outside the relationship
- Seek professional help if you suspect you're being gaslit
