## DESIGN PRINCIPLES (YouTube Thumbnails):

1. **Text size:** Large enough to read on mobile (40% of screen)
2. **Colors:** High contrast (yellow on black, white on red)
3. **Faces:** Avoid showing real faces (legal issues)
4. **Symbols:** Use evidence, objects, courtroom elements
5. **Emotion:** Dark, ominous, intriguing
6. **Clutter:** Minimal - 1-2 elements max + text
7. **Brand:** Consistent style across all Mind Crimes videos
