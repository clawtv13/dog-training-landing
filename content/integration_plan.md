## Plan for Integrating Missing Assets & Details

This document outlines the process for integrating the final details needed to complete the Cooling Relief Cap Shopify project.

### 1. Product Images
*   **Requirement:** High-quality product images are needed for the e-commerce listing.
*   **Action:** Once provided (e.g., as files or URLs), these images will be placed in the workspace (e.g., a new directory like `/static/images/cooling-relief-cap/`).
*   **Integration:** The image paths in the product template (`/root/.openclaw/workspace/content/shopify_pages/cooling_relief_cap_product_template.md`) will be updated to point to the actual image file locations or URLs.

### 2. Privacy Policy Company Details
*   **Requirement:** Specific company information is needed for the Privacy Policy.
*   **Action:** The user will provide the company name, address, and phone number.
*   **Integration:** These details will replace the placeholders `[Your Company Name]`, `[Your Company Address]`, and `[Your Company Phone Number]` within the Privacy Policy file: `/root/.openclaw/workspace/content/shopify_pages/privacy_policy.md`.

Once these steps are completed, the product listing and policy pages will be fully finalized and ready for implementation in Shopify.
