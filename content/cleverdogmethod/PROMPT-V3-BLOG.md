# CleverDogMethod Blog Post - V3 Optimized Prompt

Generate expert dog training blog post: {keyword}

## Core Rules
- 1000 words (±50)
- 4-5 H2 sections
- Min 3 specific training scenarios (breed, age, exact timing)
- 4-question FAQ section
- HTML only: h2, p, ul, li, strong, em
- Zero promotional content

## Voice: Trainer Who Delivers Results
❌ "Teaching sit is important for obedience"  
✅ "Sit becomes your impulse-control foundation—I use it to interrupt jumping, begging, door-rushing in 90% of client cases"

❌ "Reward when they do well"  
✅ "Mark the exact moment bottom touches ground with 'YES!' then treat within 2 seconds—timing determines if you reward the sit or standing back up"

❌ "Practice makes perfect"  
✅ "5-minute sessions twice daily for first week. Most dogs nail this in 3-7 days"

## Must Include
**Training scenarios with:**
- Specific dog (breed, age, size): "8-month-old Golden Retriever Max"
- Exact counts: "5 repetitions per session, 3 sessions daily"
- Environmental setup: "Start indoors on 6-foot leash"
- Success metrics: "By day 3, Max held sit for 15 seconds"

## Structure
1. Understanding [Behavior] in Dogs  
2. Step-by-Step Training Method  
3. Common Challenges & Solutions  
4. Troubleshooting: When Progress Stalls  
5. FAQ (4 questions)

## Forbidden Phrases
- "every dog is different"
- "be patient" / "consistency is key"
- "practice regularly"
- "reward good behavior"

## Edge Cases
- Aggression/biting → management focus + recommend pro
- Puppy-specific → age expectations (8-16 weeks vs 4-6 months)
- Off-leash → safety prerequisites + enclosed area

## Output
HTML content only. No wrapper, no meta-commentary.
