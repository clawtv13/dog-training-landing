# Puppy Training Checklist: First 30 Days

**Complete Day-by-Day Guide | Clever Dog Method™**

Your first 30 days set the foundation for a lifetime. This checklist ensures you don't miss critical training windows.

---

## Days 1-7: Homecoming Week

### Day 1: Arrival Day
☐ Create safe space (playpen/gated area)
☐ Remove hazards (cords, toxic plants, small objects)
☐ First potty break outside immediately
☐ Introduce crate with treats/toys
☐ Feed first meal (continue breeder's food)
☐ Establish bedtime routine
☐ Expect accidents—normal!

**Goal:** Safety + settling in

### Day 2: Name Recognition
☐ Name game: Say name → Treat when they look (20x)
☐ Potty breaks every 2 hours
☐ Track eating/potty times
☐ Short explore session (5-10 min)
☐ No visitors yet (too overwhelming)

### Day 3: Crate Introduction
☐ Feed meals in crate (door open)
☐ Toss treats in randomly throughout day
☐ First short crate session: 30 seconds door closed
☐ Always potty right after crate time
☐ Cover crate with blanket for naps

### Day 4: Handling Practice
☐ Touch paws → treat (10x)
☐ Touch ears → treat (10x)
☐ Gentle restraint: Hold 5 seconds → release + treat
☐ Mock nail trim (don't cut yet)
☐ Look at teeth/gums

**Why:** Vet visits, grooming, nail trims

### Day 5: Bite Inhibition Begins  
☐ When puppy bites: "Ouch!" → Turn away 10 seconds
☐ Always redirect to toy
☐ Reward toy-biting heavily
☐ No hands as toys rule

### Day 6: Socialization (Sounds)
☐ Play "puppy socialization sounds" YouTube (low volume)
☐ Treat while sounds play
☐ Vacuum for 2 minutes (treat throughout)
☐ Doorbell sounds → treat

### Day 7: First Vet Visit
☐ Schedule appointment if not done
☐ Bring treats for vet office
☐ Get vaccination schedule
☐ Discuss deworming, flea prevention
☐ Ask about pet insurance

**Week 1 Checklist:**
☐ Name recognition 70%+ response rate
☐ Potty schedule established  
☐ Sleeping through night (or 1 wake-up)
☐ Crate = happy place

## Days 8-14: Basic Training Begins

### Day 8: "Sit" Command
☐ Lure with treat: nose up → butt down
☐ Mark "Yes!" when butt touches floor
☐ Treat immediately
☐ Repeat 20 times
☐ Practice in 3 different rooms

### Day 9: "Come" Command  
☐ Squat down, open arms, excited voice
☐ Say "come!" or puppy's name
☐ Treat + praise when they arrive
☐ Never call to punish/do something unpleasant
☐ Practice 15 times today

### Day 10: Leash Introduction
☐ Let puppy drag leash indoors (supervised)
☐ Pick up leash, walk 5 steps, treat
☐ Don't pull if they freeze—wait patiently
☐ 2-3 short sessions (3 minutes each)

### Day 11: Surface Exposure
☐ Walk on grass (treat)
☐ Walk on concrete (treat)
☐ Walk on tile/wood floor (treat)
☐ Walk on gravel (treat)
☐ Build positive associations

### Day 12: Meeting People
☐ Invite calm friend over
☐ Guest ignores puppy initially
☐ When puppy sits → Guest pets gently
☐ Teach: Jumping = ignored, Sitting = attention
☐ Keep sessions short (10 min)

### Day 13: "Leave It" Game
☐ Treat in closed fist
☐ Puppy will paw/mouth hand
☐ Wait in silence
☐ When they back off → open hand + treat
☐ Repeat 20 times

### Day 14: Review Week
☐ Test "sit" (success rate: __%)
☐ Test "come" (success rate: __%)  
☐ Leash walking improving?
☐ Potty accidents this week: ____
☐ Plan Week 3 focus areas

## Days 15-21: Expanding Skills

### Day 15: "Down" Command
☐ From sit position
☐ Lure treat to ground
☐ When elbows touch: "Yes!" + treat
☐ 15 repetitions

### Day 16: "Stay" Introduction
☐ Ask for sit
☐ Hold hand up (stop signal)
☐ Wait 2 seconds
☐ Step back 1 step
☐ Return, treat
☐ Gradually increase time/distance

### Day 17: Car Rides
☐ Sit in parked car (5 min)
☐ Treats for calm behavior
☐ Short drive around block
☐ End somewhere fun (park?)
☐ Goal: Car = good things

### Day 18: Alone Time Practice
☐ Leave room for 30 seconds
☐ Return before puppy panics
☐ Repeat 10 times
☐ Gradually increase duration
☐ Prevents separation anxiety

### Day 19: Vet Visit #2
☐ Vaccinations
☐ Weight check
☐ Health questions
☐ Treats throughout visit
☐ Socialize in waiting room (if vaccinated)

### Day 20: First Walk Outside
☐ Short distance (5-10 min)
☐ Carry puppy if not fully vaccinated
☐ Let them sniff everything
☐ Treat for walking nicely
☐ Avoid dog parks until vaccines complete

### Day 21: Week 3 Assessment
☐ Commands mastered: ____________
☐ Potty training success rate: ____%
☐ Socialization experiences: _____
☐ Areas needing work: ___________

## Days 22-30: Polishing & Problem Prevention

### Day 22: Drop It / Trade Game
☐ Puppy has toy
☐ Offer treat
☐ When they drop toy → "Drop it!" + treat
☐ Give toy back (teaches trading is safe)

### Day 23: Grooming Practice
☐ Brush for 2 minutes (treat every 10 seconds)
☐ Touch paw pads (preparing for nail trims)
☐ Wipe face with damp cloth
☐ Look in ears

### Day 24: Impulse Control  
☐ "Wait" before meals: Bowl down → Wait 3 seconds → "Okay!"
☐ "Wait" at doorways: Open door → Sit-stay → "Okay!"
☐ Teaches patience

### Day 25: Recall Games
☐ "Puppy ping-pong": 2 people call back and forth
☐ Hide and seek: Hide, call puppy, jackpot when found
☐ Never punish when they come

### Day 26: Public Outing
☐ Pet-friendly store (if vaccinated)
☐ Carry puppy or walk on leash
☐ Lots of treats for calm behavior
☐ Expose to: carts, crowds, sounds
☐ Keep it short and positive

### Day 27: Play Date
☐ With vaccinated, gentle adult dog
☐ Supervised closely
☐ Watch for: play bows, taking breaks
☐ Separate if too rough
☐ Teaches bite inhibition

### Day 28: Training Challenge Day
☐ Practice all commands in new location
☐ Add distractions (toys, food on ground)
☐ Test "stay" with you out of sight (10 seconds)
☐ Reward heavily for success

### Day 29: Nighttime Routine Locked In
☐ No water 2 hours before bed
☐ Last potty at 11pm
☐ Crate in bedroom or nearby
☐ Should sleep 6-8 hours now
