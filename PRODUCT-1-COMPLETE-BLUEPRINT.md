**Blueprint 1: Invoice Processing Automation**

**Problem:** 4 hours/week processing invoices manually

**Solution:** ChatGPT + Zapier extracts data → Google Sheets → QuickBooks

**ROI:**
- Time saved: 4h/week = 16h/month
- Cost saved: $640/month (vs VA at $40/hour)
- Setup time: 30 minutes
- Payback: Instant

**Tools:**
- ChatGPT (free tier works)
- Zapier (free plan: 100 tasks/month)
- Google Sheets (free)
- QuickBooks (if you use it)

**Setup:**

Step 1: Create Google Sheet template
- Columns: Date, Client, Amount, Status, Invoice #
- Share with Zapier

Step 2: Zapier Trigger
- Email received → Gmail watch folder "Invoices"

Step 3: ChatGPT Extraction
- Prompt: "Extract from email: invoice number, client name, amount, due date. Return as JSON."
- Connect ChatGPT API (or use Make.com ChatGPT module)

Step 4: Sheet Update
- Zapier action: Add row to Google Sheets

Step 5: QuickBooks Integration (optional)
- Zapier action: Create invoice in QuickBooks

**Testing:**
- Send 3 sample invoice emails
- Verify data extracted correctly
- Check Sheet populated
- Validate QuickBooks entry

**Optimization:**
- Add automatic categorization (consulting vs product)
- Flag overdue invoices (due date passed)
- Auto-send payment reminders
