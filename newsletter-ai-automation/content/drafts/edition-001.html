⚡ Automation Workflow: AI-Powered Customer Support Triage

Problem: Spending 2+ hours daily sorting support emails instead of building your product.

Tools needed: Gmail, OpenAI API ($0.002 per request), Zapier ($49/month)

Steps:

  • Zapier monitors your support inbox for new emails
  • Sends email content to OpenAI with prompt: "Categorize as: Bug, Feature Request, Billing, or General. Rate urgency 1-10. Suggest response."
  • Creates labeled tickets in your system with AI suggestions
  • Auto-responds to simple questions, flags urgent issues

Result: Cut support triage from 2 hours to 15 minutes daily. Handle 80% of questions automatically. Cost: ~$25/month including API calls for 500 emails.
