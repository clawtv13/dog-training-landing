Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: March 2024

At Clever Dog Method, we believe in complete transparency. This page explains how we earn money and how it affects the content you see on our website.

We Are an Affiliate Website

Clever Dog Method is a participant in affiliate marketing programs. This means that when you click on certain links on our website and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you.

How It Works

Here's what happens when you click an affiliate link on our site:

  • You are redirected to the product vendor's website
  • If you make a purchase, the vendor pays us a small commission
  • The price you pay is exactly the same as if you went directly to the vendor
  • The commission comes out of the vendor's profits, not your pocket

Our Affiliate Partnerships

We are currently affiliated with:

  • ClickBank: A trusted online retailer of digital products. ClickBank is responsible for processing payments, handling refunds, and customer service for products purchased through our links.

Why We Use Affiliate Links

Running a website costs money — hosting, domain registration, content creation, and maintenance all add up. Affiliate commissions help us:

  • Keep the website running and ad-free
  • Continue providing free, valuable content
  • Research and review new dog training products
  • Improve the website experience

Our Commitment to You

Even though we earn commissions, we are committed to:

  • Honesty: We only recommend products we genuinely believe can help dog owners
  • Transparency: We clearly disclose our affiliate relationships
  • Quality: We prioritize products with good reputations and money-back guarantees
  • Your Best Interest: Our recommendations are based on what we believe will help you, not what pays us the most

FTC Compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's 16 CFR Part 255, "Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising."

Questions?

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or this disclosure, please contact us.
