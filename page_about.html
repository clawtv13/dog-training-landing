About Calmora

Calmora is a focused self-care brand built around one simple idea: fast, reusable cooling relief for overheated, overstimulated, high-friction days.

Why we exist

Most quick-cooling solutions are messy, awkward, or annoying to use. We wanted something better: a soft wraparound cooling cap that stays in place, feels comfortable, and fits easily into daily life.

That is why we created Cooling Relief Cap.

What we believe

We believe relief should be simple, not complicated. No batteries. No cords. No setup. Just chill it, pull it on, and reset whenever you need 10–20 minutes of cool, calm quiet.

Who it is for

Calmora is for people who spend long hours on screens, deal with overheated or overstimulated days, prioritize self-care routines, and want a low-effort reset tool they can keep ready at home, at work, or while traveling.
