**AFTER (3,901 chars, objective rubric):**
```
SCORING FRAMEWORK (0-40 scale):

RELEVANCE (0-10):
10: Directly applicable to solo founder automation workflow
 7: Related but requires adaptation
 4: Tangentially relevant
 0: Off-topic or enterprise-only

NOVELTY (0-10):
10: First coverage of new development (<24h), actionable implications
 7: New angle on existing topic
 4: Rehashed content with minor updates
 0: Duplicate or widely covered

ACTIONABILITY (0-10):
10: Implement within 1 hour with clear ROI
 7: Implement within 1 week with moderate setup
 4: Requires significant time/cost investment
 0: Theoretical or research-only

IMPACT (0-10):
10: Saves 10+ hours/week OR generates $1K+/month
 7: Saves 3-5 hours/week OR generates $200-500/month
 4: Marginal efficiency gain
 0: No measurable business impact

EXAMPLES:

Score 38: "I built AI agent that automates invoice processing - saves 8h/week, code included"
- Relevance: 10 (perfect fit)
- Novelty: 9 (specific implementation)
- Actionability: 10 (code provided)
```

### Master Content SEO (RANKINGS FIX):

**BEFORE (264 chars, basic):**
```
"Provide primary keyword"
"Secondary keywords (5-8)"
"Search intent"
"Optimized title"
```

**AFTER (3,888 chars, comprehensive):**
```
SEO RESEARCH PROTOCOL:

KEYWORD RESEARCH:

Primary keyword:
- Exact phrase (2-4 words)
- Search volume: Use Google Trends or Ahrefs estimate
- Competition: Analyze top 10 SERP results
- Target: Medium volume, low-medium competition
- Density target: 1.2-1.8% in final content

Secondary keywords:
- 8-12 related terms (not just synonyms)
- Long-tail variations (3-5 words)
- LSI keywords (semantically related)
- Include: Tool names, technique names, problem phrases

SEARCH INTENT ANALYSIS:
- Informational: "how to", "what is", "guide to"
- Commercial: "best", "vs", "review", "comparison"
- Transactional: "buy", "price", "deal"
- Navigational: "[brand name]"

COMPETITOR GAPS:
Analyze top 5 ranking articles:
- What do they cover well?
- What do they miss entirely?
- What could be explained better?
- What updates are they missing? (2023+ developments)

INTERNAL LINKING:
Identify 3-5 opportunities:
- Related topics we've covered
- Anchor text (natural, not forced)
- Context where link fits organically

TITLE OPTIMIZATION:
- 55-60 characters (Google preview limit)
- Primary keyword in first 30 chars
- Include number or trigger word
- Front-load value ("How to..." not "... How to")

Example:
❌ "A Guide to Using AI Automation in Your Business"
✅ "AI Automation for Solopreneurs: 5 Tools Under $50"

META DESCRIPTION:
- 150-160 characters
- Primary keyword once
- Clear value proposition
- Call to action
- Compelling reason to click

Example:
❌ "Learn about AI automation tools and how they can help your business grow."
✅ "Cut 15h/week with these 5 AI tools ($50/month). Solo founder tested. Implementation guide included."
```

**Why this matters:**
- SEO was guesswork
- Now systematic research protocol
- Better keyword targeting
- Improved click-through rates

## 💡 KEY LEARNINGS

### What Made V3 Better:

1. **Specificity > Generality**
   - "Professional tone" → 3 voice examples
   - "800-1200 words" → "1000 words (±100)"

2. **Examples > Instructions**
   - "Use clear language" → ❌ Bad vs ✅ Good samples

3. **Constraints > Freedom**
   - MUST/SHOULD/AVOID hierarchy
   - Explicit anti-patterns

4. **Validation > Trust**
   - Show valid format
   - Show invalid formats
   - List validation rules

5. **Efficiency > Completeness**
   - Bullets instead of paragraphs
   - Remove filler words
   - Focused requirements
