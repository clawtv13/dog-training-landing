## 🎨 Landing Page Elements

### Structure
1. **Hero** - Benefit-driven headline + CTA
2. **Problem** - List pain points they relate to
3. **Solution** - Introduce product + differentiators
4. **Benefits** - What they'll get (outcomes, not features)
5. **How It Works** - 3-4 simple steps
6. **What's Included** - All deliverables
7. **Social Proof** - Testimonials + stats
8. **Pricing** - Clear price + value comparison
9. **FAQ** - Address objections
10. **Guarantee** - 30-day money-back
11. **Final CTA** - Last chance to buy

### Key Principles
- Mobile-responsive design
- Fast loading (no heavy images)
- Single CTA (don't distract)
- Trust signals (guarantee, testimonials)
- Urgency (limited time)

## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Perfectionism** - Done > perfect. Launch fast, iterate.
2. **Underpricing** - Charge for value, not cost.
3. **No urgency** - Always have a reason to buy NOW.
4. **Generic copy** - Speak to ONE person, not everyone.
5. **Ignoring feedback** - Your customers tell you what to build.
