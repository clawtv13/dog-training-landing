# 7 Additional Automation Blueprints

Blueprints 4-10 save an additional 18 hours/week. Combined with the 3 critical blueprints, you're looking at 30+ hours automated monthly.

## Blueprint 4: Social Media Scheduler

**The Problem:** Posting manually 5x/week = 2 hours (finding content, writing captions, uploading, timing). Miss a day and engagement drops.

**ROI: $320/month saved** (8h × $40/hour)

**Setup (20 minutes):**

**Tools:** Buffer or Hypefury (free tier), ChatGPT, Notion

**Step 1:** Create content bank in Notion
- Column A: Content idea/link
- Column B: Platform (Twitter/LinkedIn/both)
- Column C: Scheduled date
- Column D: Status

**Step 2:** Batch-generate captions
Use **Prompt #12: Social Media Repurposing** from Part 2
- Turn 1 blog post → 10 social posts
- Store in Notion

**Step 3:** Connect Buffer/Hypefury
- Import captions from Notion
- Schedule optimal times (Buffer suggests best slots)
- Queue fills automatically

**Step 4:** Set cadence
- Twitter: 2x/day (10am, 4pm)
- LinkedIn: 1x/day (8am)

**Result:** Post 14x/week with 1 hour/week effort (vs 2h/week manual)

## Blueprint 5: Email Triage System

**The Problem:** 47 unread emails every morning. Takes 30 min to sort urgent vs FYI. Inbox zero feels impossible.

**ROI: $800/month saved** (20h × $40/hour)

**Setup (25 minutes):**

**Tools:** Gmail filters + ChatGPT + Labels

**Step 1:** Create Gmail labels
- URGENT (reply today)
- IMPORTANT (reply this week)
- FYI (archive/read later)
- WAITING (pending someone else)

**Step 2:** Use **Prompt #1: Email Triage Agent**
- Run daily on inbox
- Copy-paste 20-30 subject lines
- ChatGPT categorizes in 3 minutes

**Step 3:** Setup Gmail filters (learn your patterns)
After 30 days, automate common senders:
- Boss emails → URGENT
- Newsletter subject contains "update" → FYI
- Subject "invoice" → IMPORTANT

**Step 4:** Process in batches
- URGENT: Handle 8am-9am
- IMPORTANT: Friday afternoon block
- FYI: Archive or scan Sunday

**Result:** 30 min/day → 10 min/day (15 min saved = 5h/week)

## Blueprint 6: Research Assistant

**The Problem:** Spend 4 hours/week gathering competitor data, market research, trend analysis. Manual Google searches, screenshot chaos.

**ROI: $640/month saved** (16h × $40/hour)

**Setup (30 minutes):**

**Tools:** Perplexity AI or ChatGPT + Notion

**Step 1:** Create research database in Notion
- Columns: Topic, Source, Key Findings, Date, Action Items

**Step 2:** Use **Prompt #21: Competitor Analysis Deep Dive**
- Input: Competitor website URLs
- Output: Pricing, positioning, features, gaps
- Time: 5 min vs 45 min manual

**Step 3:** Setup weekly research tasks
- Monday: Competitor pricing check (10 min)
- Wednesday: Industry news scan (15 min)
- Friday: Customer pain points from support tickets (10 min)

**Step 4:** Store in Notion with tags
- Tag by: Product, Feature, Urgency
- Link to relevant projects

**Result:** 4h/week research → 45 min/week (3.25h saved)

## Blueprint 7: Customer FAQ Bot

**The Problem:** Answering same 10 questions 20x/week. "What's your refund policy?" "How do I reset password?" Eating 6 hours/week.

**ROI: $960/month saved** (24h × $40/hour)

**Setup (35 minutes):**

**Tools:** ChatGPT + Crisp/Intercom chat widget

**Step 1:** Compile your top 20 questions
- Check support tickets
- Ask ChatGPT: "What are common questions for [your business type]"

**Step 2:** Write answers (use **Prompt #29: FAQ Generator**)
- Input: Your support tickets
- Output: FAQ with answers in your voice

**Step 3:** Setup Crisp chatbot
- Free plan includes basic bot
- Add FAQ responses
- Trigger: Keyword detection ("refund" → shows refund policy)

**Step 4:** Route complex questions to you
- Bot handles 60% automatically
- 40% escalate to human (you)

**Result:** 6h/week support → 2.5h/week (3.5h saved)

**Real example:** Mike's SaaS bot handles:
- Password resets (link to docs)
- Billing questions (link to FAQ)
- Feature requests (captured in Notion, replied "logged")
- 60% resolution rate, 0 human time

## Blueprint 8: Content Repurposing

**The Problem:** Write 1 blog post (4 hours), use once. Should be 10 pieces of content but takes another 3 hours to repurpose.

**ROI: $480/month saved** (12h × $40/hour)

**Setup (20 minutes):**

**Tools:** ChatGPT + Canva (free tier)

**Step 1:** Use **Prompt #12: Social Media Repurposing**
- Input: 1,200-word blog post
- Output: 10 social posts (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram captions)
- Time: 8 minutes vs 90 minutes manual

**Step 2:** Create templates in Canva
- Quote cards (lime + dark design)
- Stat graphics (1080×1080)
- Thread images (Twitter format)

**Step 3:** Batch process
- Write blog Monday
- Repurpose Tuesday (30 min for all 10)
- Schedule rest of week Wednesday

**Format outputs:**
- 1 blog → 10 tweets
- 1 blog → 3 LinkedIn posts  
- 1 blog → 5 quote graphics
- 1 blog → 1 email newsletter section

**Result:** 1 blog = 19 pieces of content in 30 min (vs 3 hours)

## Blueprint 9: Calendar Management

**The Problem:** 10 emails/week rescheduling meetings. "Can we move Tuesday to Wednesday?" "What time works for you?" 2 hours/week of calendar Tetris.

**ROI: $320/month saved** (8h × $40/hour)

**Setup (25 minutes):**

**Tools:** Calendly (free tier) + Zapier + ChatGPT

**Step 1:** Setup Calendly availability
- Working hours: 9am-5pm (buffer 30 min lunch)
- Meeting types: Discovery (30 min), Deep Dive (60 min), Quick Call (15 min)
- Buffer between meetings: 15 min

**Step 2:** Create smart booking rules
- Max 3 meetings/day
- No meetings before 10am (focus time)
- No back-to-back 60-min calls

**Step 3:** Use **Prompt #6: Calendar Conflict Resolver**
When someone requests time you're blocked:
- Input: Their requested times + your availability
- Output: 3 alternative times with reasoning
- Send as reply

**Step 4:** Auto-reschedule when canceled
- Zapier: When Calendly canceled → send "Rebook" email
- Include Calendly link
- 80% self-reschedule

**Result:** 2h/week scheduling → 15 min/week (1.75h saved)

## Blueprint 10: Expense Tracking

**The Problem:** Receipts in email, wallet, photos. Month-end = 1 hour categorizing for accounting. Annual tax prep nightmare.

**ROI: $160/month saved** (4h × $40/hour)

**Setup (20 minutes):**

**Tools:** ChatGPT + Google Sheets + Phone camera

**Step 1:** Create expense sheet
Columns: Date, Vendor, Amount, Category, Payment Method, Receipt Link

**Step 2:** Use **Prompt #38: Expense Categorizer**
- Take photo of receipt
- Upload to ChatGPT (or describe)
- Output: Categorized expense as JSON
- Paste into Sheet

Categories:
- Software subscriptions
- Office supplies
- Travel
- Meals (client vs personal)
- Marketing
- Professional services

**Step 3:** Setup rules
- <$50: Log but don't keep receipt (photo backup only)
- >$50: Upload receipt to Google Drive folder
- >$500: Flag for accountant review

**Step 4:** Monthly export
- Sheet → Export CSV
- Send to accountant
- Takes 5 minutes vs 60 minutes manual sorting

**Result:** 1h/month accounting → 15 min/month (3h/quarter saved)

**Real example:** Sarah categorizes 40 expenses/month
- Before: 20 min/week sorting receipts
- After: 5 min/week (ChatGPT auto-categorizes from photo)
- Accountant bill: $200/month → $120/month (less cleanup needed)
