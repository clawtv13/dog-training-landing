## 📧 Email Outreach Strategy

**Sequence:**
1. **Day 0:** Initial email (personalized, value-focused)
2. **Day 3:** Follow-up #1 (add social proof)
3. **Day 7:** Follow-up #2 (last ask, request intro)

**Conversion Tips:**
- Research each company (recent launches, pain points)
- Show proof (screenshots of traffic/engagement)
- Start small ($500 → easier to close, upsell later)
- Personalize (no generic templates)
- Build relationships (not just transactions)

**Target:** 10 emails/day = 300/month = 30-50 replies = 5-10 deals
