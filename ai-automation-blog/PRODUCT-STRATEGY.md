## 💰 Pricing Strategy

### Value-Based Pricing Formula

```
Price = (Time Saved × $50/hr) + (Money Impact / 10) + Premium Factor
```

**Example: AI Automation Starter Kit**
- Time saved: 20 hours × $50 = $1,000 value
- Money impact: $5,000 potential / 10 = $500 value
- Total value: $1,500
- Price: $29 (1.9% of value = no-brainer)

### Pricing Psychology

1. **Charm Pricing** - Use $X9 or $X7 (not $X0)
   - $29 not $30
   - $197 not $200

2. **Anchor Pricing** - Show "was" price
   - $29 ~~$49~~ (Save $20)

3. **Bundle Discount** - 30-40% off individual
   - 3 products: $245 → $149

4. **Scarcity Mechanics**
   - Launch pricing (7 days only)
   - Limited bonuses (first 50 buyers)
   - Spots (cohort courses)

### A/B Testing Plan

**Test 1: Price Points**
- Variant A: $29
- Variant B: $27
- Metric: Total revenue

**Test 2: Anchor Pricing**
- Variant A: Just $29
- Variant B: $29 (was $49)
- Metric: Conversion rate

**Test 3: Payment Plans**
- Variant A: Full payment only
- Variant B: 2x $99 option
- Metric: Total buyers

## 📄 Sales Page Framework

### Structure (Long-Form)

1. **Hero**
   - Benefit-driven headline
   - Subheadline (value prop)
   - CTA button
   - Trust badge (guarantee)

2. **Problem (PAS Framework)**
   - Sound familiar? (list pain points)
   - Agitate (what happens if not solved)
   - Transition to solution

3. **Solution**
   - Introduce product
   - Key differentiators
   - Proof it works

4. **Benefits (Not Features)**
   - What they get (transformation)
   - Icon + headline + description
   - Focus on outcomes

5. **How It Works**
   - 3-4 simple steps
   - Remove complexity anxiety

6. **What's Included**
   - List all deliverables
   - Show total value vs. price

7. **Social Proof**
   - Customer testimonials (3-5)
   - Results/stats
   - Photos if possible

8. **Pricing**
   - Clear price
   - Compare to value
   - CTA button

9. **FAQ**
   - Address objections
   - Build trust
   - Remove friction

10. **Guarantee**
    - 30-day money-back
    - No questions asked
    - Reduce risk

11. **Final CTA**
    - Restate transformation
    - CTA button
    - Urgency reminder

### Copywriting Frameworks

**PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)**
- Best for: Problem-aware audience
- Structure: Pain → Worse → Fix

**AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)**
- Best for: Cold traffic
- Structure: Hook → Info → Want → Buy

**Before-After-Bridge**
- Best for: Transformation focus
- Structure: Now → Future → Path

## ⚠️ Risk Mitigation

### Potential Pitfalls

**1. Creating Products Nobody Wants**
- **Solution:** Validate with audience first. Ask what they struggle with.

**2. Perfectionism (Never Launching)**
- **Solution:** Set hard deadlines. Done > perfect.

**3. Underpricing**
- **Solution:** Start with value-based pricing. Can always lower later.

**4. Poor Launch Execution**
- **Solution:** Follow launch checklist religiously.

**5. No Audience**
- **Solution:** Build list first. Need 100 engaged subscribers minimum.

### Contingency Plans

**If Product 1 Flops:**
- Analyze feedback deeply
- Pivot positioning or pricing
- Create different product type

**If Revenue Below Target:**
- Double down on list building
- Create more low-ticket products
- Increase launch frequency

**If Time Constrained:**
- Focus on low-ticket only
- Delay mid/high-ticket
- Use templates more
