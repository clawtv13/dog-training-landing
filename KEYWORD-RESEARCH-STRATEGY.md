## 💡 PRO TIPS:

**1. "Answer The Exact Question":**
- If keyword is "how to automate email follow-ups"
- Title: "How to Automate Email Follow-Ups (Step-by-Step Guide)"
- NOT: "Email Automation Best Practices"

**2. "Satisfy Search Intent":**
- Tutorial keyword → Tutorial format
- Comparison keyword → Comparison table
- Question keyword → Direct answer first

**3. "Steal From Success":**
- Find top-ranking posts for your keywords
- Analyze their structure
- Create BETTER version (more depth, better examples)

**4. "Content Refresh Strategy":**
- After 30 days, update posts with:
  - New examples
  - Updated stats
  - More internal links
  - Better CTAs
- Google loves fresh content

**5. "Keyword Clusters":**
- Group related keywords
- One pillar post (3,000+ words)
- 5-10 supporting posts (1,500-2,000 words)
- All internally linked
- Dominates entire topic cluster
