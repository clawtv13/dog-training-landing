## Email List Segmentation & Automation

### Segments to Build:
1. **Purchasers** → Onboarding + engagement sequences
2. **Email leads (pre-purchase)** → Educational + conversion sequences
3. **Engaged (opened 3+ emails)** → Upsell + referral sequences
4. **Unengaged (0 opens in 60 days)** → Win-back or sunset sequences

### Automation Workflows:
- **Post-purchase:** Welcome → Check-in → Engagement → Referral → Re-engagement
- **Abandoned cart** (if e-commerce): Reminder + incentive after 24h, 3d, 7d
- **Browse abandonment:** If customer viewed product page but didn't buy
- **Seasonal campaigns:** Holiday + summer sequences

## Email Best Practices (Calmora Brand)

### Tone
- Warm, friendly, founder voice
- Not corporate. Not salesy. Real.
- Admit imperfection. Show humanity.

### Length
- Short when possible (2–3 paragraphs for engagement)
- Longer when valuable (onboarding, guides)
- Mobile-first design (most opens on phone)

### CTA
- One main CTA per email (max two)
- Button + text link (increase click rate)
- Clear action: "Shop Now," "Reply Below," "Leave a Review"

### Frequency
- Not more than 1 email every 3 days for engaged users
- More frequent for new purchases (build habit)
- Less frequent for lapsed users (respect inbox)

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate (target: 30%+ for engaged, 15%+ for cold)
- Click rate (target: 5%+ for engaged, 2%+ for cold)
- Conversion rate (target: 2%+ for email visitors)
- Unsubscribe rate (keep <0.5%)
