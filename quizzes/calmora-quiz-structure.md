**Headline:**

```
Your Sleep Type: The Overthinker
```

**Description:**
```

This is caused by an overactive sympathetic nervous system (fight-or-flight mode). Your body isn't getting the signal that it's safe to sleep.
```

### **TYPE 4: The Anxious Sleeper**

**Headline:**
```
Your Sleep Type: The Anxious Sleeper
```

**Description:**
```
Bedtime feels stressful. You worry about not sleeping, which makes it worse. You might have physical symptoms — tight chest, racing heart, or restless legs.

This is sleep anxiety — a feedback loop where worrying about sleep prevents sleep. Your nervous system is stuck in "alert mode."
```

**What Works for You:**
```
✅ Box Breathing (Before Bed)
   → Inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 4s, hold 4s × 5 rounds

✅ Progressive Muscle Relaxation
   → Tense/release each muscle group (feet → head)

✅ Weighted Blanket (15-20 lbs)
   → Deep pressure = calms nervous system

✅ "Non-Sleep Deep Rest" (Yoga Nidra)
   → 10-20 min guided meditation (even if you don't sleep, your body rests)
```

```
Your Sleep Type: The Night Owl
```
