## PASO 1: Crear Form en Tally

1. Tally.so → "Create new form"
2. Start from scratch

---

## PASO 2: Title Block

**Add block → Title**

**Title:**
```
Can't Sleep? Take Our Free 2-Minute Sleep Assessment
```

**Description:**
```
Discover your Sleep Type and get a personalized plan to fall asleep faster — backed by science.
```

---

## PASO 3: Questions (Add 7 question blocks)

### **Q1: Multiple Choice**
**Question:**
```
What's your biggest struggle when trying to sleep?
```

**Options:**
- My mind races with thoughts and worries
- I fall asleep fine but wake up at 3-4 AM
- I sleep 7-8 hours but still wake up exhausted
- I feel anxious or restless when I try to sleep
- I'm naturally alert late at night (night owl)

**Settings:** Required ✅

---

### **Q2: Multiple Choice**
**Question:**
```
On average, how long does it take you to fall asleep?
```

**Options:**
- Less than 15 minutes
- 15-30 minutes
- 30-60 minutes
- More than 1 hour
- I don't even know anymore

**Settings:** Required ✅

---

### **Q3: Multiple Choice**
**Question:**
```
Which best describes your bedroom at night?
```

**Options:**
- Dark, cool, and quiet
- Bright (street lights, devices)
- Noisy (traffic, neighbors, snoring)
- Too warm or too cold
- I use screens before bed

**Settings:** Required ✅

---

### **Q4: Multiple Choice**
**Question:**
```
What do you usually do in the hour before bed?
```

**Options:**
- Scroll social media or watch TV
- Work or check emails
- Read or meditate
- Exercise or eat heavy meals
- No consistent routine

**Settings:** Required ✅

---

### **Q5: Linear Scale (1-10)**
**Question:**
```
How would you rate your stress/anxiety level right now?
```

**Scale:** 1 to 10

**Labels:**
- Min (1): Calm and relaxed
- Max (10): Extremely stressed

**Settings:** Required ✅

---

### **Q6: Checkboxes (Multi-select)**


**Options:**

- Exercise during the day
- Meditation or breathing exercises
