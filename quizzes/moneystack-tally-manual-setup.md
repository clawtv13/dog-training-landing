## PASO 1: Crear Form

Tally.so → "Create new form" → Start from scratch

---

## PASO 2: Title Block

**Title:**
```
Are You Making These Money Mistakes?
```

**Description:**
```
Take our 2-minute Money Type Quiz and discover the #1 thing holding you back financially.
```

---

## PASO 3: Questions (Add 7 question blocks)

### **Q1: Multiple Choice**
**Question:**
```
Where are you right now with money?
```

**Options:**
- Not investing at all (scared to start)
- Following Dave Ramsey / paying off debt
- Saving but not investing (keeping cash)
- Investing but chasing trends (FOMO)
- Already investing consistently (want to optimize)

**Required:** ✅

---

### **Q2: Multiple Choice**
**Question:**
```
What's your approximate annual income?
```

**Options:**
- Under $40K
- $40K - $75K
- $75K - $150K
- $150K - $300K
- Over $300K

**Required:** ✅

---

### **Q3: Multiple Choice**
**Question:**
```
How much debt do you have (excluding mortgage)?
```

**Options:**
- No debt
- Under $5K
- $5K - $20K
- $20K - $50K
- Over $50K

**Required:** ✅

---

### **Q4: Multiple Choice**
**Question:**
```
How many months of expenses do you have saved?
```

**Options:**
- Less than 1 month
- 1-3 months
- 3-6 months
- 6-12 months
- Over 12 months

**Required:** ✅

---

### **Q5: Linear Scale (1-10)**
**Question:**
```
How confident are you about investing?
```

**Scale:** 1 to 10

**Labels:**
- Min (1): No idea where to start
- Max (10): I could teach others

**Required:** ✅

---

### **Q6: Multiple Choice**
**Question:**
```
What scares you most about money?
```

**Options:**
- Not having enough for retirement
- Losing money in the market
- Never getting out of debt
- Missing out on opportunities (FOMO)
- Not knowing if I'm doing it "right"

**Required:** ✅
