**Your Action Plan:**
```

   → Personal loans (10%+)
   
   ✅ PAY MINIMUM (Invest the Rest):
   → Student loans (3-5% interest)
   → Car loan (2-4%)
   → Mortgage (3-4%)

💰 STEP 2: 50/50 Split Strategy
   → 50% of "extra money" to high-interest debt
   → 50% to Roth IRA (VTI/VOO)

   → $250 to investment

📊 STEP 3: Run The Math
   → Credit card: $10K at 18% = $1,800/year cost
   → Investment: $3K/year at 10% = $300/year gain
   → Net: You're STILL better off investing while paying debt

🚫 AVOID:
   ❌ Dave Ramsey's "no investing until debt-free" (bad math)
   ❌ Paying off low-interest debt aggressively
```
